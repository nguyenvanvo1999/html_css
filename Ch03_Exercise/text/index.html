Our Mission

San Joaquin Valley Town Hall is a non-profit organization that is run by an all-volunteer board of directors. Our mission is to bring nationally and internationally renowned, thought-provoking speakers who inform, educate, and entertain our audience! As one of out members told us:

"Each year I give a ticket package to each of our family members. I think of it as a gift of knowledge...and that is priceless."
